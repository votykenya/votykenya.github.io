The Voice of Youth (VOTY) is a Non-Profit, Community Based Organization founded in the year 2005 based in Kendu-Bay town, Homabay county. The organization was created to support; women, youth and individuals with different abilities living in the region. This support is in terms of Health, education, food security and industrialization as income generating which is archieved through VOTY's strong advocacy program. Â
